Product Overview

This cream focuses on repair, softness, and barrier support for skin that feels dry, tight, rough, or less stable. With high-concentration pro-xylane and a soothing repair complex, it is designed to help skin maintain a fuller, softer, and more comfortable finish while fitting naturally into a daily routine.

How to Use

  • Apply after toner, serum, or essence as the cream step in your routine.
  • Spread evenly across face and neck with gentle upward motions.
  • Use morning or evening according to the richness level preferred in your routine.
