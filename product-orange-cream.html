Product Overview

This cream is designed around antioxidant care, brightness, and a lighter skin feel for people who dislike overly rich moisturizers. With idebenone, niacinamide, and an antioxidant complex, it helps skin maintain a clearer, smoother, and more refined-looking finish while fitting comfortably into a daily routine.

How to Use

  • Apply after toner, serum, or essence as the cream step in your routine.
  • Spread evenly across face and neck with gentle upward motions.
  • Use morning and evening, especially when skin looks dull or feels slightly tired.
